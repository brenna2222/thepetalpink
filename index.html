The Ultimate Brunch Destination

Farm to Table Fare.

The Pink Petal is a brunch spot focusing on a unique, aesthetic, and overall delicious experience. We aim to be the ultimate brunch destination where guests can eat incredible food and get a photo for their Instagram. Being located in downtown San Diego they aimed to create an escape from the busy city life with a funky yet sleek interior prominent in the art deco style. Serving farm to table food in collaboration with a local farm.
